Where the Art of Photography Meets You.

Photography Collected with Intention

The Gallery Austin is a curated online fine art photography gallery presenting seasonal collections of limited edition archival prints. We are not a marketplace. We are not a print shop. We are a gallery — in the full meaning of that word.

Founded in Austin, Texas in 2026, The Gallery operates on a simple principle: exceptional photography deserves an exceptional home. Each work is offered as an archival pigment print, limited to an edition of ten. No more are ever produced.

How We Work

Works are not sold through a cart or a button. Collectors inquire. Our curator responds personally within 24 hours. The conversation begins there.

Photographers exhibit by invitation and application. We review each submission seasonally. Approved artists show their work at no cost. When a work sells, The Gallery takes a 35% commission — the collector receives a numbered certificate of authenticity signed by the artist.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Gallery Austin?
The Gallery Austin is a curated online fine art photography gallery based in Austin, Texas. We present seasonal collections of limited edition archival prints across eleven halls. Photography collected with intention.
How are works sold at The Gallery Austin?
Works are not sold directly through the site. Collectors inquire about specific works and our curator responds personally within 24 hours. Each edition is limited to 10 archival pigment prints. Once an edition closes, no further prints are produced.
What types of photography wall art does The Gallery Austin offer?
The Gallery Austin offers fine art photography wall art prints across eleven halls: Portraits, Urban, Abstract, Nature, Coastal, Sacred, La Femme, L'Homme, Allegro, La Gente, and Discovery. All works are available as large format archival pigment prints in limited editions of ten.
Can photographers exhibit at The Gallery Austin?
Yes. Photographers may apply to exhibit seasonally. Applications are reviewed by our curators. If accepted, works are exhibited at no cost — The Gallery takes a commission on sales only.
Are there fine art photography galleries in Austin, Texas?
The Gallery Austin is an Austin-based fine art photography gallery operating online, presenting seasonal collections to collectors across the United States and worldwide. Visit thegalleryaustin.net to explore the current collection.
