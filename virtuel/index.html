What is a Digital Edition?

A digital edition from The Gallery Austin is a unique NFT — a non-fungible token — minted on the Tezos blockchain. It is a cryptographically verified certificate of ownership for a specific digital artwork.

Each edition is numbered. Each is limited to 10. When edition 10 is claimed, no further digital editions of that work will ever be produced.

How it works

Connect your Tezos wallet. Select a work. Choose your edition. Complete your collection. The NFT is minted and delivered to your wallet immediately upon payment.

You receive a digital certificate of authenticity — on-chain, permanent, and verifiable by anyone, anywhere, forever. No intermediaries. No waiting.

The blockchain

Virtuel editions are minted on Tezos — an energy-efficient, proof-of-stake blockchain favored by the fine art community. Transaction fees are negligible. The environmental footprint is minimal.

Physical + Digital

Virtuel editions are separate products from The Gallery Austin's physical fine art prints. They are different worlds — different collectors, different experiences, different ownership. Both are limited to 10. Both are permanent.
